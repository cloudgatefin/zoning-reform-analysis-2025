## Key Findings

### Most Common Reforms
1. **ADU/Lot Split** (72%) - Dominant reform type
2. **Comprehensive Reform** (17%) - Full zoning overhauls
3. **Zoning Upzones** (8%) - Density increases
4. **Parking Reform** (3%) - Minimum eliminations

### Geographic Distribution
- West Coast dominates (CA, OR, WA = 60% of cities)
- Mountain West emerging (MT, CO = 8%)
- Southeast underrepresented
- Northeast with individual city reforms

### Temporal Trends
- **2019-2020**: Minneapolis 2040, Oregon HB2001 passage
- **2021-2022**: California SB9, major implementations
- **2023**: Washington HB1110, Montana "Miracle"
- **2024**: NYC City of Yes, Colorado ADU reform

### States with Statewide Mandates
1. California - SB9 (all cities)
2. Oregon - HB2001 (cities 10k+)
3. Washington - HB1110 (cities 25k+)
4. Montana - SB323/528 (statewide)
5. Colorado - SB24-106 (ADU preemption)
